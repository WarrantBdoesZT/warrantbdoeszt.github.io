👤 About

I'm a cybersecurity professional focused on Zero Trust architecture, working at the intersection of DoD security frameworks, network segmentation, and identity-centric defense. My background spans building and assessing Zero Trust implementations against NIST 800-207, NIST SP 1800-35, and the DoD Zero Trust Reference Architecture — from misconfiguration audits to full architecture design and adversary simulation.

The tools on this site come out of that work: interactive visualizers and assessment utilities meant to make Zero Trust concepts and DoD requirements easier to teach, explore, and apply. Connect with me on LinkedIn.

🏛️ DoD Zero Trust — 7 Pillars

The DoD Zero Trust Reference Architecture organizes 152 Zero Trust activities across seven pillars — User, Device, Application & Workload, Data, Network/Environment, Automation & Orchestration, and Visibility & Analytics.
